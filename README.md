# ⚡ EV Charging Demand Forecasting & Analysis (Nashik)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project focuses on analyzing and forecasting Electric Vehicle (EV) charging demand in Nashik using simulated (dummy) data. The analysis incorporates time-based patterns, area-wise demand, and weather conditions to predict future charging requirements.

The goal is to understand how EV charging demand varies across different regions and external factors, enabling better planning of charging infrastructure.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
- Analyze EV charging demand patterns across different areas in Nashik.
- Forecast charging demand for the next 30 days
- Study the impact of weather conditions on EV usage
- Identify high-demand locations for optimized station placement
- Provide data-driven insights for EV infrastructure planning

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies Used
- Python (Pandas, NumPy)
- Data Visualization (Matplotlib / Seaborn)
- Time Series Forecasting
- Jupyter Notebook / VS Code
- Power BI

---

## 📂 Dataset
- Dummy dataset created for Nashik EV charging stations
- Includes:
  - Charging station locations
  - Timestamp (date & time)
  - Energy demand (kWh)
  - Area-wise distribution
  - Weather conditions (temperature, etc.)

---

## 📊 Analysis & Methodology

### 1. Data Preparation
- Generated synthetic dataset for Nashik
- Cleaned and structured data for analysis
- Extracted features such as:
  - Hour
  - Day
  - Month
  - Area

---

### 2. Demand Analysis
- Analyzed EV charging demand across:
  - Different times of day
  - Various locations
- Identified peak usage hours and high-demand zones

---

### 3. Weather-Based Analysis
- Studied how weather conditions affect EV usage
- Correlated temperature and demand patterns
- Observed variation in charging behavior based on environmental factors

---

### 4. Demand Forecasting
- Forecasted EV charging demand for the next 30 days
- Used time-series based approach
- Predicted future load trends for better infrastructure planning

---

## 📈 Key Insights
- EV demand varies significantly by time of day (peak hours observed)
- Certain areas in Nashik show consistently higher demand
- Weather conditions influence charging behavior
- Forecasting helps anticipate future infrastructure needs

---

## 📌 Conclusion
This project demonstrates how data analysis and forecasting techniques can be used to understand and predict EV charging demand. The insights can help optimize charging station placement and improve energy distribution strategies.
